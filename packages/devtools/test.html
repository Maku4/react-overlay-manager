Overlay Manager DevTools Test

Instructions:

  1. Click the buttons below to open different overlays
  2. Look for the floating "Overlays" button in the bottom-right corner
  3. Click the floating button or press Ctrl+Shift+O to open DevTools
  4. In the DevTools panel, you can:
    • See all active overlays with their visibility status
    • Click on an overlay to see its details
    • View the props passed to each overlay
    • Close or remove overlays directly from DevTools
